About Brain Tumor Types:

Glioma: Originates in glial cells that surround nerve cells. Usually aggressive and fast-growing.

Meningioma: Forms in the meninges (membrane surrounding the brain). Often slow-growing and benign.

Pituitary: Develops in the pituitary gland. Can affect hormone production.
